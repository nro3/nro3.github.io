The outbreak of the novel coronovirus COVID-19 changed the world drastically. It is a highly infectious respiratory illness spread through water droplets when an infected person coughs or sneezes. Throughout the pandemic, various strategies have been implemented by governments worldwide to contain the spread of the disease. This visualization focuses on the response of the Illinois government to the COVID-19 pandemic.
